Key Outcomes

What We've Achieved

Every number represents a real girl whose life has been positively impacted.

📚

Knowledge Transformation

110 girls now understand menstrual cycles, proper hygiene practices, and when to seek medical help. They can distinguish facts from myths about their bodies.

💪

Increased Confidence

Girls who received education and hygiene kits report feeling more confident attending school during their periods and discussing health topics openly.

🏫

Reduced Absenteeism

Schools we visited report fewer absences among girls who participated in our program, keeping girls in the classroom where they belong.

🩹

Healthier Practices

Girls are now practicing proper intimate hygiene, reducing their risk of preventable infections and improving their overall health and well-being.

Our Journey

Timeline of Achievements

🏆

Grant Awarded

Selected as a recipient of the Global Youth Mobilization Youth Empowerment Fund to implement the "My Body, My Care" project.

🤝

Healthcare Partnerships Established

Partnered with qualified healthcare professionals to develop medically accurate, age-appropriate education content.

🏫

First School Visit Completed

Conducted our first education session at a secondary school in Ile-Ife, our founding community, reaching girls with vital health information.

🎒

110 Girls Educated

Reached the milestone of educating 110 adolescent girls across three secondary schools in rural communities.

🎁

110 Hygiene Kits Distributed

Provided 110 girls with hygiene kits containing essential products for safe, dignified menstrual management.
