Our Founding Partner

Youth Empowerment Fund

My Body, My Care is proudly funded through the Youth Empowerment Fund, a partnership between the European Union and the Global Youth Mobilization.

The Youth Empowerment Fund provides direct financial support for youth-led initiatives focusing on the implementation of the Sustainable Development Goals at the local level. It is a flagship initiative of the EU's Youth Action Plan, designed by, with and for young people.

Since its inception, the Youth Empowerment Fund has invested more than US$5 million to support over 640 youth-led projects, engaging more than 600,000 young people and reaching over 3.6 million people across more than 125 countries.

The Fund is delivered through the Global Youth Mobilization (GYM), an initiative of the Big Six Youth Organizations, the world's largest youth-serving organizations, involving more than 250 million young people globally.
